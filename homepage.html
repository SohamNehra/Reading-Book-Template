Our Clubs

Literary Circle

Bring your own book and flip pages with new people.
IBC brings a revolutionary change in book club meetings with a virtual set up to connect and greet people around the country and books.
So, if you are looking for a book club to join but are too lazy or shy to go out, join a virtual connecting space. The book club is open for all kinds of book lovers, young, old, avid readers, or first-timers.

Young Reader

Ever wondered about a book club for kids?
No? Then start now.
It’s more inspiring and motivating to read the same book with your friends.
When kids join the book club, the appetite for reading a book starts with a never-ending hunger for discovering a lot of stories.
